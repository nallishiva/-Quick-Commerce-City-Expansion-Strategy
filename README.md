# Quick Commerce City Expansion Strategy
## Zepto & Blinkit — India Market Analysis | April 2026

### Project Summary
An end-to-end Business Analyst project identifying 
high-priority expansion cities for Zepto and Blinkit 
across India using pincode-level public data.

**Core Question:**
- Where should Zepto enter next?
- Where should Blinkit expand as a first mover?

---

### Key Findings

**Zepto:**
- Zepto operates in only 10 cities while Blinkit 
  covers 137 districts
- Karnataka leads with 548 gap pincodes where 
  Blinkit operates but Zepto does not
- Ernakulam (Kochi) is the #1 priority district — 
  142 pincodes, Blinkit confirmed, 73% internet 
  penetration, zero Zepto presence

**Blinkit:**
- Tamil Nadu has the largest No Coverage 
  opportunity — 1,412 pincodes with zero quick 
  commerce
- Kerala dominates top districts — Thrissur (168), 
  Palakkad (139), Alappuzha (113) all have no 
  Blinkit or Zepto
- 67% of urban India has zero quick commerce 
  coverage

### Data Sources
| Source | URL | What it provides |
|--------|-----|-----------------|
| India Post Pincode Data | data.gov.in | Pincode, district, state, lat/long |
| Zepto Delivery Areas | zepto.com/s/del-areas | Cities and areas Zepto serves |
| Blinkit City List | blinkit.com/press | All cities Blinkit operates in |
| TRAI Internet Data | data.gov.in | State-wise internet penetration |

---

### Methodology
Coverage analysis is at city and district level.
Individual delivery area boundaries within cities
may vary. Data verified April 2026.

**Opportunity Score Formula:**
- Coverage Gap: 50% weight
- Internet Penetration: 30% weight  
- State Development Index: 20% weight
